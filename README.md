## What This Is

An interactive data visualization tracking the coaching staffs, player development professionals, and support personnel who prepared the 2026 WNBA Draft class for the professional level.

The editorial angle: the head coach gets the credit. This viz gives credit to everyone else.

Live tonight at **[theblacktechie.github.io/wnba-draft-coach-tree](https://theblacktechie.github.io/wnba-draft-coach-tree)**

---

## The Viz

**`wnba-draft-coach-tree.html`**

- All 15 official WNBA Draft invitees organized by court position (Paint / Wing / Perimeter)
- Tap any player card to reveal the coach behind them, their role, and a one-line editorial POV
- Live Edit mode — enter actual pick numbers as they're called during the broadcast
- School filter + position tabs for easy navigation
- LIVE indicator that auto-activates 7:00–9:30 PM ET April 13, 2026
- Mobile, tablet, and desktop responsive

**Design system:** Inter font · WNBA orange (#FF6B35) · School hex colors · Athletic minimalist aesthetic

## How to Use Live Edit Tonight

1. Open the viz on your phone or laptop
2. Tap **LIVE EDIT** when the draft begins
3. As each pick is called, type the pick number into the player's card
4. Tap **LIVE EDIT** again to exit when done
5. The LIVE indicator activates automatically at 7:00 PM ET and turns off at 9:30 PM ET
